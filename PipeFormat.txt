General Format: Value, delimiter, value, delimiter, etc. 
    - After each value, a delimiter is printed.
        There will be a '&' (delimiter) after each number, status, verse, etc. 


Request Pipe Format: BookNumber&ChapterNumber&VerseNumber&
    Example: 1&1&1&
        Read as: Genesis 1:1
    2&22&13 = Exodus 22:13


Reply Pipe Format: SuccessStatus&BookName&ChapterNumber&(VerseNumber)&VerseText&
    Example: 0&Genesis&1&(1)&In the beginning...

    Output end result:  Genesis 1 (1) In the beginning...
                        (2) ... 
                        (3) ...

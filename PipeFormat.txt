Request Pipe Format: BookNumber&ChapterNumber&BookNumber
    Example: 1&1&1
        Read as: Genesis 1:1
    2&22&13 = Exodus 22:13


Reply Pipe Format: SuccessStatus&BookName&ChapterNumber&(VerseNumber)&VerseText
    Example: SUCCESS&Genesis&1&(1)&In the beginning...

    Output end result: Genesis 1 (1) In the beginning...
